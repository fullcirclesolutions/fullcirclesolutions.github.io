What is Saucery?

Saucery is a DesiredCapabilities factory component that you reference from your Maven or .NET test projects to make automated testing with SauceLabs easy.

Saucery also:

  • Handles Flow Control - so you don't send to many tests to SauceLabs at once and they don't get rejected.
  • Communicates test outcomes back to SauceLabs - Passed or Failed on the daahboard is better than Finished.
  • Handles various settings that respect your precious testing minutes thus maximising them.
  • Provides human-readable console message.
  • Includes utility classes to help with writing tests and PageObjects.

With all this plumbing taken care of by Saucery, you and your team can be more focussed writing the tests to satify the business needs of your application.
