What is Saucery?

Saucery is a component that you reference from your Maven or .NET test projects that makes automated testing with SauceLabs a breeze.

With Saucery, you and your team:

  • Have the freedom to focuson writing your test cases.
  • Will be unconcerned about "test infrastructure"or "test plumbing"code.
  • Can specify what platforms you want without worrying howit's achieved.
