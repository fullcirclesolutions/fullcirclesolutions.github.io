Why Saucery?

Why Saucery?

Executive Summary (for Managers)

With all this plumbing taken care of by Saucery, you and your team can remain focussed on writing the tests to satisfy the business needs of your application.

Technical Details (for Developers)

Saucery provides:

  • A SauceLabs DesiredCapabilities factory that just works so you don't have to think about it. Check out the How To page.
  • Dynamic Flow Control management ensuring no tests are ever dropped on the floor again due to parallel-limit rejections.
  • Outcome Management back to SauceLabs - Passed or Failed on your dashboard is better than Finished.
  • Account Minute Management for you out of the box that respect your precious testing minutes thus maximising them.
  • Human-readable console messages.
  • Utility classes to make writing tests and PageObjects easier.
