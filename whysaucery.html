Why Saucery?

Why Saucery?

For Managers

Saucery saves you time and money by providing:

  • Automatic Cross-Device Testing that make your team more productive and keeps them focussed on testing your application.
  • Assurance that all tests are submitted smoothly.
  • Test Outcome Management so developers don't have to do this.
  • Technical Utilities that mean your team can write tests faster.

With all this plumbing sorted, your team remain focussed on delivering to the business through greater test coverage across as many devices as you want.

For Developers

Saucery provides:

  • A SauceLabs DesiredCapabilities factory that just works so you don't have to think about it. Check out the How To page.
  • Dynamic Flow Control ensures tests are submitted respecting the parallellsm limit (including support for REST API limits) of your SauceLabs account.
  • Test Outcome Management - Passed or Failed on your SauceLabs dashboard is better than Finished.
  • Human-readable console messages because you're a human. Let Saucery be the machine for you.
  • Utility classes to make writing tests and PageObjects easier.
