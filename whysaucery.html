Why Saucery?

Why Saucery?

I'm a Manager

With all this plumbing taken care of by Saucery, you and your team can remain focussed on writing the tests to satisfy the business needs of your application.

I'm a Developer

  • A SauceLabs DesiredCapabilities factory that just works so you don't have to think about it. Check out the How To page.
  • Dynamic Flow Control ensuring no tests are dropped on the floor due to parallel-limit rejections.
  • Outcome Management - Passed or Failed on your dashboard is better than Finished.
  • Account Minute Management that maximises your precious test minutes.
  • Human-readable console messages because your a human. Let Saucery be the machine for you.
  • Utility classes to make writing tests and PageObjects easier.
