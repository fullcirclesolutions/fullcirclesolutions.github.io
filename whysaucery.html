Why Saucery?

Why Saucery?

Executive Summary (for Managers)

Time and Money

With all this plumbing taken care of by Saucery, you and your team can remain focussed on writing the tests to satisfy the business needs of your application.

Technical Details (for Developers)

Saucery for NUnit is a DesiredCapabilities factory for SauceLabs that you reference from your .NET test projects. Check out the How To page.

Saucery also:

  • Handles Flow Control (NUnit 3 Edition) - so you don't send too many tests to SauceLabs at once thus avoiding parallel-limit rejections.
  • Communicates test outcomes back to SauceLabs - Passed or Failed on your dashboard is better than Finished.
  • Handles various settings for you out of the box that respect your precious testing minutes thus maximising them.
  • Provides human-readable console messages.
  • Includes various utility classes to make writing tests and PageObjects easier.
